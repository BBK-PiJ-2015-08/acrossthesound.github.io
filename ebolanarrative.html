Ebola Narrative visualizing the spread of the outbreak

Project Description

For this project, which was created by Simon Johnson, I have been updating the country level and region level data as the outbreak continues, as well as finding news stories to add to the timeline. Most recently, I rewrote a function to make the way the regions of the map shade in more representative of the data.

This visualisation was republished by Le Temps.
